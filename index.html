About Me

Hie, my name is Audrey and i am a Ux design student at Uncommon.org.I am passionate about creating intuitive and user-freindly experiences, and i am skilled in a variety of design tools.I am also a certified Scratch teacher, and i love helping kids learn to code. As an UX designer , i work with clients to research and test user experiences , create information architecture, design user interfaces and test usability. My work results in improing user experiences, increasing engagement and reducing customer support costs.I am able to make a meaningful impact on the companies i work with by making their products and services easier to use and more enjoyable to interact with.v
